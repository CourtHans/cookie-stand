Pat's Scrumptious Salmon Cookies

Delicious salmon cookies, just the way Grandma made 'em!

The most unusual (but delicious!) cookie you've ever had!

You've never tasting anything quite like it! Our delectable salmon cookies were a long-time family secret until Pat decided to share them with the world! Each batch is lovingly hand-crafted using only the finest, sushi-grade Copper River Salmon. The salmon is ground into a fine paste, mixed with a unique blend of warm spices, and baked in fun salmon shapes. Each cookie is then frosted with a complimentary cream-cheese frosting. About Us

When she was just a girl, Pat Fishscale always looked forward to visits to her grandmother's house so she could enjoy Grandma Darla's legendary salmon cookies. As Pat grew older, her grandmother taught her all the secrets to this melt-in-your-mouth treat. In 2019, Pat and her partner Martin decided to launch Pat's Salmon Cookies. Their kids Nemo and Dory love helping run the family business!
